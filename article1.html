Our Local Med Ocean Heros

Our Med Ocean Hero's have been hard at work, removing plastic along Gibraltar's coastline. Todays net plastic recovered was a whopping 5kg, by two young boys, Aj and Alex Stagnetto. The two brothers, born and bred in Gibraltar, have formed a part of TNP since the beginning and have thus earned their titles as Med Ocean Heros. Through this initiative we hope to encouage like minded peers to engage in conserving and protecting our environment. In recent years, Gibratlar's coastline has been subjected to enourmus antroporgenic disurbance; construction, littering and bunkering. Our efforts will ensure that the harmful effects of these practices are minimised and will ensure that they are undertaken in a responsible and educated manner.
